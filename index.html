Why Choose Meridius Health

q. As a caregiver, can I choose my own schedule?
a.

We offer flexible schedules for most

of our non-skilled caregiving roles. Whether you

are looking for full-time employment or PRN,

we try our hardest to accommodate your schedule

with the needs and desires of the client.

q. When can we start services if my loved one is already on the waiver program?
a.

Individuals already participating with Community

Health Choices (CHC) or OLTL Waiver programs

may start as soon as 24h from the time of transfer

with Meridius Health or when we receive

the Service Authorization from the members

insurance plan.

q. Is Meridius Health contracted with Community Health Choices?
a.

Yes! Meridius is a contracted provider with every

Managed Care Organization under CHC, including

Keystone First, Amerihealth Caritas, PA Health

and Wellness, and UPMC. We also participate

with OLTL Home and Community Based Services

(HCBS) under OBRA and ACT 150 waivers

q. Does Meridius Health offer any benefits for their employees?
a.

Yes! All our employees are eligible for free

healthcare, that will include dental and vision

at an additional cost. We also offer employee funded

401k, free life insurance up to $10,000.00

per full time employee, highest pay rate in your area.
